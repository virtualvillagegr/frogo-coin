# TNT Terrain Flattener - Minecraft Bedrock Edition v1.20.26+\n\nA Minecraft Bedrock Edition mod that adds a special **Terrain Flattener TNT** that destroys all blocks above a specified Y level, creating a perfectly flat surface for building!\n\n## 🌟 Features\n\n🏔️ **Smart Terrain Flattening** - Removes all blocks above your target Y level  \n🎮 **Easy to Use** - Right-click on a block at your desired Y level  \n📦 **Large Area Coverage** - Flattens a 60×60 block area (30 blocks radius)  \n⚡ **Quick Fuse** - 4-second fuse timer before flattening begins  \n💥 **Visual Effects** - Explosion particles and sounds for feedback  \n🏗️ **Perfect for Building** - Great for preparing terrain for construction  \n🎨 **Custom Texture** - Unique visual design for the Terrain Flattener TNT  \n📚 **Crafting Recipes** - Two ways to craft the item (Redstone & Diamond patterns)\n\n## 📖 How to Use\n\n1. **Craft or Obtain** the Terrain Flattener TNT using one of the recipes:\n   - **Recipe #1**: Redstone Block (4) + Gold Block (4) + TNT (1)\n   - **Recipe #2**: Diamond Block (4) + Gold Block (4) + TNT (1)\n\n2. **Hold** the item in your hand\n3. **Look** at a block at the Y level you want to flatten to (e.g., Y=25)\n4. **Right-click** to place the TNT\n5. **Wait** 4 seconds for the fuse to burn and the terrain to flatten\n6. **Build** on your new flat surface!\n\n## 📦 Installation\n\n### For Minecraft Bedrock Edition 26.23+\n\n1. Extract the behavior pack and resource pack folders\n2. Place them in your world's datapacks directory:\n   - **Windows**: `AppData\\Roaming\\.minecraft\\datapacks`\n   - **macOS**: `~/Library/Application Support/minecraft/datapacks`\n   - **Android**: Internal Storage → Games → com.mojang → minecraftWorlds → [World Name]\n\n3. Activate both packs in world settings\n4. Enable \"Experimental Features\" if prompted\n5. Create or load your world\n\n## 📋 Crafting Recipes\n\n### Recipe #1 - Redstone Pattern (Recommended)\n```\nR = Redstone Block\nG = Gold Block\nT = TNT\n\n  R G R\n  G T G\n  R G R\n```\n\n### Recipe
